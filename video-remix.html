Video Remix

Artist Statement

Sometimes we find the art we need to make when we try to make art, we thought we wanted to or should make. This video remix is a pivot, a move away from a piece that I am passionate about, but I was not able to distill the fine points I wanted to make in that video at this time.

One of my passions is mountain biking and I always have some mountain biking videos in my feed. The opening line of The Ridge â€“ Danny Macaskill, "I always wondered if it would be possible for me to ride my mountain bike up there", hooked me and made me think of the hundreds of times I have looked out my car window. Thinking oh there is a line there and one there. My skill is significantly less than is required to ride these lines I form in my mind, but this opportunity gave me a chance to share what I feel it would be like to ride these raw challenge filed lines if I could ride them.

Hope you enjoy!
