Artist Statement

The design idea behind this Zine was to find all the lost text in my house. I want to find the oldest text I could, and I use it. Additionally, I wanted to commemorate some of the lost family members I have and so I dedicated a page to them. I think the blurred look on the obituaries and in memorandum cards shows that despite how much they were loved and missed time fades their memories. There are handwritten notes from the oldest and youngest family members. I love looking at signatures and have several signatures ranging from professional signatures by an artist and a Naval Captain. I feel this project means a lot to me as all the found text is from my families past or tied to my lifeâ€™s journey and that this Zine hopefully will make people think of their families and help them cherish the written artifacts that they have from their other family members.
